Our Story

From the peaks of the Himalayas to your cup, discover the journey of authentic Nepali coffee culture

Where Mountains Meet Coffee

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Kathmandu's vibrant Thamel district, Himalayan Coffee House was born from a passion to showcase the world's finest high-altitude coffee beans while celebrating Nepal's rich cultural heritage.

Our journey began when our founder, a mountaineer and coffee enthusiast, discovered the exceptional quality of coffee grown in Nepal's mountain regions. We realized that these beans, cultivated at altitudes above 1,200 meters, possessed a unique flavor profile that deserved global recognition.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between traditional Nepali hospitality and world-class coffee culture, creating a space where locals and travelers can experience the authentic taste of the Himalayas.

From Farm to Cup

High-Altitude Excellence

Our coffee beans are sourced from small-scale farmers in the Gulmi, Palpa, and Syangja districts of Nepal, where coffee plants thrive at altitudes between 1,200 to 1,800 meters above sea level.

The cool mountain climate, rich soil, and careful cultivation methods result in beans with exceptional flavor complexity, featuring notes of chocolate, caramel, and subtle floral undertones.

Fair Trade Partnership

We work directly with local farmer cooperatives, ensuring fair prices and supporting sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and preserve traditional cultivation methods.

Every cup you enjoy contributes to the livelihood of Nepali farming families and helps maintain the pristine mountain ecosystems where our coffee grows.
